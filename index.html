Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made as a project for Text as Data II, taught at the University of Antwerp.

This website was developed by Sebastiaan Peeters under the Creative Commons license.

This digital edition was based on the Shelley Godwin Archive and uses the scanned versions of the manuscript kept at the Bodleian Library. It was encoded according to the Beckett Digital Manuscript Project manual.
